Privacy Policy – Fitness Log

Last Updated: May 31, 2026

Fitness Log is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using the Fitness Log application.

1. Information Collection

Fitness Log only stores information that users voluntarily enter into the app, such as meals, calorie records, and fitness-related data.

2. No Third-Party Sharing

Fitness Log does not sell, rent, trade, or share user data with any third party, advertiser, marketing company, or external organization.

3. User Control

Users have full control over their data and may edit or delete their information within the app at any time.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

5. No Sale of Personal Information

Fitness Log does not sell personal information or user data for commercial purposes.

6. Purpose of Data Usage

Any information stored in the app is used solely to provide calorie tracking, meal logging, and fitness management features.

7. Limited Permissions

Fitness Log only requests permissions that are necessary for the proper functioning of the application.

8. Children's Privacy

Fitness Log is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the updated version of this policy.
